    • About YEFD

    Yemen Eye Development Foundation (YEFD). A voluntary, non-governmental, non-profit organization established on 2022/2/28. It works to improve community participation in obtaining basic services

    • Our Goals
    • Our Vision
    • Our Mission
    Aims at:
    1. Supporting and developing civil society to be a healthy, positive, educated and sustainable society
    2. Coordinating, preparing and implementing relief and development projects and programmes, and cooperating with local and international organizations to achieve sustainable development goals
    3. Promoting economic and social empowerment, building individual and community capabilities, and encouraging innovation and creativity
    4. Supporting health services and improving their quality to contribute to improving the health and nutritional standards, raising the level of awareness, and building the capabilities of workers
    5. Paying attention to people with special needs (people with disabilities), integrating them into society, following up on their needs, and providing them with the necessary care.
    6. Supporting and activating scientific networks and research centers to improve the educational and research process while serving the people of society
    7. Disaster preparedness, early warning, and emergency program implementation
    8. Providing psychological support and guidance and organizing work on psychological care, which contributes to building a healthy environment and an effective society
    9. Protecting rights and freedoms to bring humanitarian services to the level of international quality to preserve human life and dignity


    A world where everyone enjoys stable and sustainable living.
    To empower individuals and communities to have equal access to basic needs and rights.
